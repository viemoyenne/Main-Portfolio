A Shot Of Whisky

Is-lay? . Eye-La?

"Whisky Knowledge +100exp
Marketing & Branding +200exp."

22 December 2020, Tuesday
wait is that a hint of chocolate & rustic wood? oh, it's just alcohol.

My first serious job I landed, whisky on the job.
I started out assisting with marketing and sales, pulling and retaining clients for the distinguished whisky brands we had to offer. Althought some might say our prices are steep, well. They actually are. I focused solely on strategizing how we were able to reach out to the younger consumers,(the wealthy ones actually)and retain the ever loyal ones(even wealthier) with different campaigns and events. Soon I progressed(?) to assisting with handling one of the many stores as the company was down sizing staff. Well assisted into brand management too. I didn't complain, since there were bound to be challenges, and challenges meant experience. I had more opportunities to meet up with not just the event hosts, direct contact suppliers, but with our clients and front-end staff as well. Understanding consumer's behavior became an action instead of a term. Oh and more tasting of expensive alcohol goes without saying.
