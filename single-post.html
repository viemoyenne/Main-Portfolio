Ready for a job

Confident . Cautious

"Not many get a chance to hit the career re-set button"
-Jason Bateman.

22 December 2020, Tuesday
"Internships? Traineeships? Full time? Part time? Freelance?"

So many platforms, so many jobs, and much more requirements.
Looking through all the requirements, it is quite intimidating and that's the truth. One thing I learnt, life doesn't come easy. I mean why not? There's definitely a company that sees the potential in you. Wether be it internships, traineeships, full time, part time, yeah sure I'll take it. Looks like a whole bunch of opportunities waiting for me. If they don't? Well I just gotta prove it. I need to learn as I go, that's one thing coding has taught me. Other than a whole lot of patience and frustration.
