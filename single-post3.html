Leadership

"I ate salmon everyday, but I enjoyed it... ...as a leader."
-Myself.

22 December 2020, Tuesday
"Customer service + outlet management + back-end operations? How hard can it be?"

Progressed into F&B after tasting too much alcohol.
It didn't seemed too difficult at first, "management" role and all. Only then I was able to fully comprehend what leadership was, and how challenging it was to do everything all in one plate. I took on a role of an Operations Manager at this place. An extension of a popular food chain held from Norway with 7 outlets and it's first in Asia. For an entire year, I handled back & front operations, hiring, maitaining QC/shipments, assisting marketing strategies and best of all, consuming salmon thrice a day.
I would say this experience humbled me overall. Be it a customer/client or simply as a manager, I got to understand both parties perspective. I'm thankful especially for this particular role given, because without it, I wouldn't be the person I am today, confident, understanding, and a well rounded leader.
