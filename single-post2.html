Self Study Journey

it's raining now.

Not gonna lie, it is tough.

15 December 2020 Tuesday
"I nearly gave up, challenges day & night, money was an issue too. Thankfully I had the right people in my life."

It was definitely a hurdle, possibly one of the toughest I have faced in my life. Doing a mid-career switch, I would ask myself often "what the hell am I doing?" at 5am. Having no income, but merely relying on the little savings I had, I guess one could say it was a good challenge to face. Indeed it was. One of the greatest motivation especially when I had my back faced against the wall, having stressed myself if I could ever do it. I started off with DataCamp, but my motivation faded. I switched to FreeCodeCamp not long after, that's where I got interested. I tried doing mini web projects, stayed up endless nights asking myself and well, Google lots of questions. I nearly gave up, challenges day & night, money was an issue too. Thankfully I had the right people in my life. I am still learning, right now even as you see this. It doesn't stop does it? One could never finish learning.
